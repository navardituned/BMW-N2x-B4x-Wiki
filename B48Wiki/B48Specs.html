B4x Engine Specifications

The BMW B4x Engine has the following critical specifications:

  • Engine Block & Cylinder Head Metal Composition: Heat-Treated All Aluminium Made From AlSiMgCu0.5
  • Crankshaft Material: Forged C38+N2 (B48E Crank is 1lbs lighter than the rest)
  • Connecting Rod Material: "Drop-Forged" Material
  • Piston Material: Cast Material (B48E has a higher silicon content)
  • Bore Spacing: 91 mm
  • Bore Size: 82 mm
  • Displacement: 1.998 L
  • Stroke: 94.6 mm
  • Compression Ratio: Either 9.5:1 (B48E) or 10.2:1 (30i Model Cars) or 11.0:1 (20i & 30e Model Cars)
  • Connecting Rod Centre to Centre: 148mm (B48E 146mm)
  • Crank Throw: TBD
  • Crankshaft Offset: 0 mm
  • Wrist Pin Offset: TBD
  • Main Caps: (B48E and B58 Share same Main Cap otherwise different)
  • Cylinder Wall Plasma Arc Coating Thickness: 0.3 mm
  • Cylinder Wall Thickness Between Cylinders: 9 mm
  • Cylinder Wall Thickness on the Thrust & Non-Thrust Sides: 9 mm
  • Intake Valve Diameter / Stem-diameter: 30.10 mm / 4.96 mm
  • Exhaust Valve Diameter / Stem-diameter: 28.5 mm / 5.95 mm
  • Maximum Valve-Lift, Intake/Exhaust Valve: 9.9 mm / 9.7 mm
  • VANOS Adjustment Range, Intake: 70 째CA
  • VANOS Adjustment Range, Exhaust: 60 째CA
  • Intake Camshaft Spread: 122.5 - 52.5 째CA
  • Exhaust Camshaft Spread: 120 - 60 째CA

  • Opening Period, Exhaust Camshaft (Duration): TBD
